Our Story

In the spring of 2002, two college roommates, Sal and Joe, began experimenting with coffee flavors in their dorm. A few months down, they asked a few classmates to try out their unique espresso drinks.

Before Sal and Joe knew it, their espresso drinks became more popular than the campus cafeteria's coffee which led Sal and Joe into opening Cup of Joe's!
